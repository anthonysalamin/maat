The Problem

A firm learns a project lost money only once it's over.

Project information is scattered across WhatsApp, email, Excel and paper — so no one has a live, trustworthy view of where a job stands or whether it is profitable.

How Work Runs Today

  • Site decisions live in WhatsApp groups
  • Budgets in personal Excel files
  • Variations & approvals buried in email
  • Delivery notes & invoices on paper
  • Accounting in a separate system, weeks behind

What It Causes

  • No real-time view of project profitability
  • Cost overruns discovered too late to fix
  • Disputes & warranty claims with no paper trail
  • "Who approved this?" — nobody can say
  • Project managers buried in admin, not on site

The Swiss reality that makes it worse

High labour costs make every wasted hour expensive · a skilled-worker shortage makes admin on the wrong people unaffordable · rising compliance burden · tight margins leave no room for a project that quietly slips into the red.

The Solution

One workspace, from intent to settlement.

MAAT is not another system — it is the layer that connects them. It links commercial intent, delivery work, commitments and settlement, so control flows from revenue to margin. It runs on phone, tablet and desktop — approvals and site capture happen where the work is.

INTENT

Sales & CRM capture commercial intent — leads, opportunities, quotations and commitments.

›

EXECUTION

Projects & procurement turn intent into delivery — phases, tasks, orders and receipts, governed by approvals.

›

OUTCOME

Finance records the result — invoices, payments, allocations, and live cost-against-revenue.

Document chain: deal → project → request → order → invoice → payment — one audit trail for every action.
